Nick Bare

Posted on May 27, 2025

I just watched Nick Bare’s video where he mentions how his phrase “go one more” was coined during a run. He had quit, but then felt the significance of the moment, returned to finish the run, and ultimately decided to go one more mile.

In my view, this was a spark of genius that, to a large extent, would determine his impact on the industry.

People hate on him, saying he’s juiced to the gills, that he’s not genuine, and they condescendingly refer to BPN-sponsored influencers as Nick Bare’s minions, and so on.

No matter what one thinks of Nick Bare—his training style, his business approach—“go one more” is such a powerful and impactful message for so many people who could be his target audience that it’s hard to deny it was a moment of exceptional creativity, simply a spark of genius.
