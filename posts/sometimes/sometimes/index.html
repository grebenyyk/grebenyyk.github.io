Sometimes

Posted on Aug 22, 2023

Sometimes I am struck by a revelation that I now live on the shores of the Pacific.

Since my early childhood I have had this strange longing for past centuries and for lands and shores I have never been to. 

I guess it has something to do with an old book of short stories from which I remember nothing but a sailor sitting in some remote place and enjoying his cheese sandwich.

When I travel the world, I always come back to memories of that sailor and the remote shores, trying to get a sense of how a particular place suits my longing. The closest I have come in the past was Santiago de Chile, and now the hills and living blocks of Shenzhen bring me even closer. 

Perhaps that sailor is me. Perhaps those distant shores are my happiness.
