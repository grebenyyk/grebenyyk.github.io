Coffee

Posted on Feb 26, 2024

Here are some particular coffees that I tasted and what I think about them

Place 好运到咖啡士多
Location 广东省深圳市福田区八卦一路619栋一层

【双重发酵日晒】哥伦比亚•希爪

Double fermentation, solar exposure. Columbia Sidra
风味：红色浆果、红樱桃、红布林、巧克力
豆种：希爪
烘焙：浅烘 light roast
Drip bag
The most unexpected and amazing coffee that i tried. I taste dry plum and cherry liquor, some nice fruit acidity, chocolate candies. Absolutely stunning.

【厌氧日晒】哥斯达黎加•蓝色火山庄园

Anaerobic solarization. Costa Rica, Volcàn Azul
风味：白葡萄酒、柑橘、红茶、菠萝、黑醋栗
豆种：ET-47
烘焙：极浅烘 ultra light roast
V60 pour over
Citrus, black tea, just a hint of pineapple. Felt a bit plain compared to the 双重发酵日晒 哥伦比亚•希爪.
