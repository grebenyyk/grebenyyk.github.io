Blogs

Life after the the lab – a 12-step plan ∙ Nobody cares about your elegant western blot from 2017 or how “this never happened in the lab”. It’s not interesting. You sound like a broken TED Talk. Rein it in or risk becoming That Guy – the one everyone avoids at the coffee machine. Write it all down in a blog post like a normal repressed adult. Then hit publish and pretend you’ve moved on.

Teacher for a year ∙ I work at an International School. This is a subspecies of the School with a few distinctive traits: desperately high staff turnover (around 30% of the teachers at my school are in their first year working there), general organizational dysfunction, and a language barrier which invalidates a solid portion of our work — it doesn’t matter how many differentiation notes I employ, I can’t do much for the kids who can neither speak nor read English. Their education is between their parents and God.

You should write ∙ Don’t edit it into a thought leadership post. Don’t tidy it up so your ex thinks you’ve grown as a person. Don’t turn it into fucking content. Just write. For no one. For nothing.
